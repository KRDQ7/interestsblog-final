More on Jungian

I find this intersting probably because of a weird game that uses some terms from it but also because it seems interesting to me. In Jungian psychology there is various terms that classify parts of the mind and what not. I do not fully comprehened it but from what I understand there are various aspects to a person including the Self, the Persona, and the Shadow. Self is the one I do not fully comprehend the meaning behind, which I suppose is because I do not fully comprehend Jungian psychology. I personally interpret it as a union of the Persona and Shadow though. The Persona is often likened to a personality that one puts on in public to brave it. Say for instance a persona of a fool that someone puts on but of one whom truthfully is miserable when by themselves. Lastly there is the Shadow which to Jung is the general evil and ill will mankind harbors or so I have heard. I personally think of the shadow as parts of people that exist that we are ashamed to admit to having. Truth be told I do not understand the concept of the Shadow too much and will not elaborate much on it as I do not fully comprehend it other than how it can be likened to things like the ID from Freudian psychology.
